## BELOW THE FOLD — Section 5: FAQ

**Section label:** FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

**Q: What happens at my first visit?**
You'll fill out a short intake form ahead of time. We'll spend 10–20 minutes on a verbal and physical assessment, then hands-on treatment for the rest of the session. You leave with real relief and a clear plan.

**Q: Do you direct bill insurance?**
We don't direct bill, but all our therapists are Registered Massage Therapists (RMTs) recognized by major insurers across Alberta. We send you a digital receipt you can submit for reimbursement — most clients get it back quickly.

**Q: How soon can I get in?**
Our therapists are in high demand. You can typically book an appointment 1–2 weeks out. Book online or call us and we'll find the soonest available slot.

**Q: Should a massage hurt?**
No. It should feel effective, not painful. There may be moments of mild discomfort when we're working to make changes in the tissue, but we always check in. Communication is key — tell your therapist if anything is too much.

**Q: Where are you located?**
213 19 St NW #4, Calgary, AB T2N 2H9 — in the West Hillhurst neighbourhood. 10 minutes from downtown with lots of free street parking.

Hours: Mon–Thu 8am–8pm · Fri 8am–5pm · Sat 10am–3pm
